Product Description

Game Ready Laptop

This metal-clad Acer Predator Helios gaming laptop features a 39.6 cm (15.6) FHD IPS display is a powerful and stylish device for your gaming needs.

NVIDIA RTX Technology

Powered by the NVIDIA Turing GPU architecture, along with NVIDIA RTX 3060, for a fast, realistic, and AI-enhanced gaming experience. The NVIDIA Turing architecture brings together real-time ray tracing, programmable shading, and artificial intelligence to improve your gaming experience.

Desktop-caliber Gaming

This gaming laptop runs on a 10th Gen processor that can deliver a clock speed of up to 5.0 GHz Turbo. The 6 cores and 12 threads are capable of overcoming most challenges while ensuring that your gaming experience is not compromised.

Fast Speed

This laptop features a 240 Hz panel that comes with a 3 ms overdrive response time. As a result, you get to view blur-free, crisp, and clear high-octane gameplay.

Quick Loading

For fast loading speed, this laptop features a dual-storage facility of 256 GB SSD, PCIe Gen3, 8 Gb/s, NVMe and 1 TB HDD 6.3 cm (2.5) with 7200 RPM. Furthermore, the expandable DDR4 storage ensures that you can customise your laptop as per your gaming needs.

Effective Cooling

This laptop comes with a 4th Gen AeroBlade 3D Technology to ensure that the system is cool while you play your favourite games.

Low Latency Gaming

For exciting and victorious online gaming, this Acer gaming laptop comes with Killer’s E2600 Ethernet Controller, Killer Wi-Fi 6 AX1650i, and Control Center 2.0.

4-zone RGB Customized Keyboard

This gaming device features a 4-zone RGB customized keyboard having a Predator typeface, concave-shaped, see-through keycaps for WASD, and two integral keys for Turbo and PredatorSense. By pressing on the Turbo key, you can activate instant overclocking, and to open the utility app, you can tap on the PredatorSense key.

Multiple Ports

This laptop comes with an HDMI 2.0, MiniDP, and the USB 3.2 standard with Gen1 and 2 ports to connect to various peripheral devices to improve your gaming experience.

Ray Tracing

This feature simulated the right behaviour of light and delivers real-time, cinematic-quality rendering. So, you can fully enjoy visually intense games.

Bionic Design

The design is inspired by powerful, yet silent owl flight mechanism, and so fans have a serrated edge to ensure that more air passes through easily.

Ultra-thin Blades

The all-metal, 0.1 mm thick 59 bionic blades have a stabilizing dual indent groove.

Reduced Noise

This gaming laptop has winglets placed along its top and bottom fan edge. Also, it has a curved fin along its inner blade. As a result, you can experience reduced turbulences and noise.

DTS X Ultra

You can enjoy immersive 360° surround sound experience on your headphones or speakers, thanks to the DTS X Ultra. So, you can enjoy spatial sound while playing games and watching videos.

Built-in Alexa

Alexa lets you control various functionalities of this gaming laptop with ease.
